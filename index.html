The challenge

Yazoo has something most brands spend decades trying to build — genuine nostalgia, deep product familiarity, and real emotional warmth. The task is not to create brand love from scratch. It's to reframe an existing relationship for an audience ready to rediscover it on their own terms.

Communications objective

  • Reignite relevance with the 16–24 year-old Gen Z audience
  • Be the market-leading brand for differentiation and innovativeness
  • Fuel brand power by tapping into Gen Z's world — authentically

Brand imagery goals

  • Reduce — "is a brand for children"
  • Increase — "is a brand I'm proud to be seen with"
  • Increase — "is a brand for people like me"
  • Increase — "is a fun brand"

Target audience

  • "Learner Adults" aged 16–24, navigating major life transitions
  • Seeking authenticity, comfort, and moments of genuine escape
  • Broad UK — priority: London, Birmingham, Manchester
  • September burst: university cities added for freshers
